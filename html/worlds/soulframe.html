SOULFRAME

WARFRAME'S FANTASY SIBLING · STILL BEING DREAMED
// WHAT IS KNOWN

THE SAME HANDS, A SOFTER WORLD

Soulframe is the next game from Digital Extremes, the studio behind Warframe. Where Warframe is chrome, speed and gunfire, Soulframe turns the dial the other way: a dreamlike fantasy world, slower and heavier combat, swords over rifles, and nature slowly reclaiming what industry took from it.

You play an Envoy, wandering the drowned forests and broken shores of Midrath. It will be free to play, like its older sibling, and it is currently playable in early access Preludes, growing patch by patch while the developers build it in the open.
